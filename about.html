About LED Media Pro

Your trusted partner for LED display solutions in Ottawa and beyond.

LED Media Pro is a leading LED solution, installation, and service company based in Ottawa, proudly serving the National Capital Region and beyond. We help businesses grow through advanced LED display technology and practical experience.

In partnership with world-leading manufacturers such as Leyard and Hikvision, we deliver high-performance LED display solutions from 0.7mm to 10mm pixel pitch for both indoor and outdoor applications. Our products serve diverse markets including retail, government, education, entertainment, sports, and enterprise sectors.

With years of expertise in LED design, installation, and maintenance, our team ensures every project is completed with precision and reliability—helping you attract attention, share your message, and create unforgettable visual experiences.
